Book Donations

Remember that time holding your first book - smelling the fresh scent of pages and caressing the words within. Well, Eastside Bookstore does, and we wish to spred that feeling to others via our Christmas charity event. Consider giving away worn novels or nostalgic works to those less fortunate than you, allowing them to experience that feeling like you did for the first time. Maybe even donate money to the cause to up the holiday joy even further.

Book Club

Have a good book that you want to share? Wish to discuss about your favorite author and their literary works? Or do you just want to enjoy a good time with company? Then, come join Eastside Bookstore's very own bookclub. We offer flexible hours, meeting from any time between Monday to Saturday, from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Our discussions can focus on any genre and any book, ranging from fantasy fiction like Harry Potter to historical classics like The Adventures of Huckleberry Finn. Best of all, each meeting comes equipped with free refreshments and a reserved space just for us. Come join us now!

Third Party Businesses

Can't find a item in our inventory, but also don't want to pay outrageous prices from other sites? No worries. We here at Eastside Bookstore are always striving to meet your needs, whether it be contacting sellers for specific items or traveling to foreign nations to obtain that one luxury object. Merely just contact us and we'll negotiate with third-party sellers for all your special needs.
